What is the most amazing photo in the world?

A school organized a fun trip for its young students,

On the way they passed through a tunnel, under which the bus driver used to pass before.

It was written on the edge of the tunnel that the height of five meters.

The height of the bus was also five meters so the driver did not stop. But this time the bus scraped against the roof of the tunnel and got stuck in the middle, horrifying the children.

The bus driver started saying "Every year I pass through the tunnel without any problem, but what happened now?

A man replied:

The road has been paved so the level of the road has been raised a bit.

There was a crowd..

A man tried to tie the bus to his car, but the rope broke every time it rubbed, some suggested bringing a sturdy crane to pull the bus, and some suggested digging and breaking it.

In the midst of these various suggestions, a child got off the bus and said, "Let's let some air out of the tires and it will start coming down from the roof of the tunnel and we will pass through safely."

Everyone was amazed by the kid's brilliant advice and actually reduced the air pressure from the tires of the bus thus the bus passed through the roof level of the tunnel and everyone came out safely.

We swell in front of people with pride, arrogance, hatred, selfishness and greed. If we remove the air of these things from inside, then it will be easier for us to pass through this tunnel of the world.

The problems are in us not in the power of our enemies
